We wanted to thank you personally for the part you’ve played helping children around the world. Together, we’ve helped more children and their families experience better health and nutrition while building economic opportunities for those living in some of the world’s most vulnerable places.

With each step forward comes the opportunity for positive transformation. And every gift, large or small, has the chance to multiply when combined with the gifts of others. We’re fans of celebrating each gesture, because giving from the heart is what love is all about.

We’re so thrilled that you’ve chosen to partner with us.

Michael Messenger President, World Vision Canada

Last year, gifts from Canadians like you helped us make a lasting difference.

And when combined with the generous contributions of the Canadian government and partners like the World Food Programme, our impact was great! Together, we shipped millions of doses of medication to keep children healthy, free of parasites and ready to excel in school. Parents and caregivers were provided with training to keep their kids well-fed, thousands of families now have access to safe drinking water and farmers learned modern techniques that will help grow their success.

Together, we’ve done amazing things around the world.

With every donation, you are improving the lives of vulnerable children. Thank you!
